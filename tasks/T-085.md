# T-085: Customer form — add 10 missing fields

**Status:** In Progress
**Assignee:** dev
**Branch:** fix/T-085-customer-missing-fields

## Objective

Add 10 fields missing from the Customer form compared to the Base44 reference:
1. Project of Interest (dropdown from projects)
2. LINE ID (text)
3. Province (text)
4. Lead Source (dropdown: Ads, Referral, Walk-in, Website, Event, Other)
5. Industry (dropdown: Manufacturing, Logistics, Retail, Technology, Real Estate, Food & Beverage, Healthcare, Other)
6. Company Size (dropdown: 1-10, 11-50, 51-200, 200+)
7. Budget Range (dropdown: Under 1M, 1-5M, 5-10M, 10-50M, 50M+)
8. Deposit Conditions (textarea)
9. Profile URL / Business Card (text URL input)
10. PDPA Consent (checkbox)
